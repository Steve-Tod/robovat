abstract move_along_gripper_path(poses, speed=None)¶

Move the arm to follow a path of the gripper.

Parameters

  • poses – The path of the gripper as a list of Pose instances.

  • speed – The maximum joint velocity.

abstract move_to_gripper_pose(pose, speed=None, timeout=None, threshold=None)¶

Move the arm to the specified gripper pose.

Parameters

  • pose – The target gripper pose, as a tuple or an instance of Pose.

  • speed – The maximum joint velocity.

  • timeout – Seconds to wait for move to finish.

  • threshold – Position threshold in radians across each joint when move is considered successful.

abstract move_to_joint_positions(positions, speed=None, timeout=None, threshold=None)¶

Move the arm to the specified joint positions.

Parameters

  • positions – The target joint positions, as a dictionary of joint_name:joint_position.

  • speed – The maximum joint velocity.

  • timeout – Seconds to wait for move to finish.

  • threshold – Joint position threshold in radians across each joint when move is considered successful.

abstract reboot()¶

Reboot the robot.

abstract reset(positions=None)¶

Reset the robot.

If the positions are not specified, the robot will go to the neutral positions.

Parameters

positions – The target joint positions, as a list or a dictionary of joint_name:joint_position.
